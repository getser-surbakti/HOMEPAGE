What Are Negative Numbers?

Negative numbers are numbers that are less than zero. You can think of them as "below zero."

or you can think of it as the opposite of the positive number

We usually use negative numbers to talk about things like temperature, money, or altitude.

    • Where Do We See Negative Numbers?

    Imagine it's winter, and the temperature is -5°C. That means it’s 5 degrees below zero!

    Negative numbers can also be used to show money that is "owed,"(hutang uang) like -$10.

    Adding Negative Numbers

    When you add a negative number, it means you are going "down."

    For example, 3 + (-2) = 1. You start at 3 and move 2 steps back to get to 1.

    Subtracting Negative Numbers

    When you subtract a negative number, it’s like adding!

    For example, 4 - (-2) = 6. You start at 4, and subtracting a negative number moves you forward to 6.

    Real-Life Examples of Negative Numbers

    1. Let’s say you have 3 apples, and then you lose 5 apples. You’d be at -2 apples! That’s a negative number because you owe apples!

    2. If the temperature drops to -5°C, it’s 5 degrees below zero! That's how we use negative numbers to represent cold temperatures.

    3. If your bank account balance is -$50, it means you owe the bank $50!

    4. The Dead Sea is at an elevation of about -430 meters. It’s below sea level, so we use negative numbers to show how deep it is.

    5. If you borrow $20 from a friend, you could say you have -$20. This represents the money you owe.

    6. Freezers are usually set to temperatures like -18°C. This means it’s cold enough to freeze food!

    7. If you dive deep into the ocean, your depth might be -200 meters. This shows how far below sea level you are!

    8. If you owe $150 on your credit card, your balance is -$150. This is the negative amount you owe the credit card company.

    9. If it’s -10°C outside, that’s really cold! We use negative numbers to describe freezing temperatures during the winter.

    10. When an airplane descends below sea level or drops below a certain height, it’s considered a negative altitude.
