1. Estimate and divide.

a. 35 ÷ 6 = ______

5 remainder 5

b. 96 ÷ 3 = ______

32

c. 123 ÷ 4 = ______

30 remainder 3

d. 248 ÷ 8 = ______

31

e. 305 ÷ 4 = ______

76 remainder 1

f. 456 ÷ 5 = ______

91 remainder 1

2. Daphne has 2 boxes of pencils. Each box has 12 pencils. She gives an equal number of pencils to the 7 members in her dance group.

a. How many pencils does each member get?

3 pencils each

b. Daphne says that she will have no pencils left. Do you agree or disagree? Why do you say so?

Disagree. There will be 3 pencils left (24 ÷ 7 = 3 remainder 3).

3. Ram has some biscuits. When he divides them equally into 5 bags, there is no remainder. When he divides them equally into 6 bags, there is a remainder of 4.

a. Ram has fewer than 100 biscuits. What is the greatest possible number of biscuits he has?

70 biscuits

b. Ram has the number of biscuits in (a). He divides them equally into 4 bags. How many biscuits will there be in each bag?

17 biscuits each
