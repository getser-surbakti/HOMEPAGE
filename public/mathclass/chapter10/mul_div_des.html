3. Factory Bean Production

a. The factory produces 125 kg of beans every day with 10 workers, each working 10 hours a day. How many kilograms does each worker pack per hour?

1.25 kg/hour

b. If production increases to 150 kg of beans every day, how many additional kilograms must each worker pack per hour?

0.25 kg/hour

c. The factory owner claims the factory produces 15.5 kg of beans per hour and runs for 10 hours every day. Is the claim correct?

No, the factory produces 125 kg of beans every day, not 155 kg.
